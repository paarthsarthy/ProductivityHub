▸ HOW TO CONNECT YOUR GOOGLE SHEET

  1. Create a new Google Sheet
  2. Row 1 headers: title channel youtube_id duration category week tip
  3. Add your videos — Claude gives you rows to paste
  4. Go to File → Share → Publish to web
  5. Choose Entire Document → CSV
  6. Click Publish and copy the URL

Paste the "Publish to web" CSV link from your Google Sheet.
